A European meeting of political methodologists.

What is PolMeth Europe?

PolMeth Europe is an annual meeting for those researching the advancement of political methodology in Europe. Established in 2021, PolMeth Europe brings together researchers who develop and apply innovative empirical methods to substantive questions across political science.

We welcome work that makes methodological contributions as well as research that applies cutting-edge empirical methods to important questions. We actively encourage participation from groups that are underrepresented in the field.
