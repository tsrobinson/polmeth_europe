The meeting

About the 2026 meeting

PolMeth Europe 2026 brings together researchers interested in innovative political methodology. We welcome proposals that focus on methodological contributions as well as work that applies cutting-edge empirical methods to research questions from all subfields. We especially encourage submissions from members of underrepresented groups.

The programme features paper presentations with discussant feedback, poster sessions for doctoral researchers, and opportunities to meet colleagues from across Europe.
